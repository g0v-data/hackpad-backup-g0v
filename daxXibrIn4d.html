* Title of Speech

Let’s don’t waste food - my efforts to start a food waste project in Taiwan

* Abstract

Worldwide billions of tons of food are wasted every year. Foodsharing is an approach from germany to save food from beeing absurdly wasted. The idea is to go to supermarkets, markets, bakeries and any other food traders shortly before they close and pick up goods the shop would normally dump.

Afterwards everybody is free to decide what he wants to do with the stuff he picked up, eat it himself, give it to friends, distribute it to people in need or bring it to the next food bank. It’s all about saving food. Nevertheless, picking up and distributing food is subjected to specific legal and social terms and conditions and requires reliable and dedicated people.

"foodsharing.de" is a well known platform to solve the issue of large supermarket want to give out useful food waste however afraid of consumer suing them if they give bad food away. This website provides possibilities to help supermarket owners to waive the responsibility and hook the people who reserve on the food can save more food and live happily.

In Taiwan, the translation of the waiver and a collaboration of German baker is on the way now. We will seek more help on this.
