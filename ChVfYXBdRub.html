We are pleased to invite you to be the keynote speaker for the g0v Summit 2016, scheduled at May 19, 2016 in Taipei, Taiwan.

[1]g0v is the civil hacking community in Taiwan focusing on information transparency.  We develop platforms and tools for the citizens to participate in society.  After the g0v Summit in 2014, for the last year our community has seen increasing collaborations from the government, mass media, and NPO/NGO.  Several attempts to introduce the practice of openness to existing institutions have resulted in high-profile projects such as a dashboard for hospital ER beds availabilities of the whole country, a community platform for amendments to national constitution, and a deliberative legislative process that runs both online and offline.

[1]: http://g0v.tw/en-US/about.html

Therefore our themes for the g0v Summit next year will be:

* How far have we gone compared to a year ago?

* Interactions with different domains, local and global politics.

We enjoy your participation in the g0v Summit 2014, and we sincerely invite you to give a keynote speech for the event next year.  You would be talking in the morning of May 19, 2016 to an audience of approximately 800 people.

Please let us know if this would be of interest to you.   Kindly reply by Dec 4, 2015 to the Chief of Conference Program, Mr. Pomin Wu, at <pomin5@gmail.com>.

Sincerely,
