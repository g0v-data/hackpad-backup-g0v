誰來過 g0v summit 2014

[6 avatars]

g0v.tw 台灣零時政府從 2012 年 10 月開始，用數位世代的思維，以開源（open source）方式號召群眾以網路世代的新模式協作，運用科技力量促進政府透明化。截至 2015 年 8 月已有 30+ 場黑客松、受邀演講 50+ 場、媒體報導 20+ 次、1000+ 遍佈三大洲的貢獻者，成果皆以自由軟體模式釋出。 2014 年 11 月舉辦零時政府高峰會更匯聚了600+ 位來自 9 國、不同領域的人參加。

2014 年的零時政府啥米 （g0v summit） 促成了許多跨領域交流合作，也開啟了許多新專案。為了分享社群中的「跨界經驗」，我們開始籌備 2016 年零時政府啥米，將邀請來自不同背景的專案參與者，分享過去一年「融合」的經驗，也讓有志運用科技改變社會的各界，能透過更多深入的溝通與交流，看看能夠發生啥米代誌。

g0v summit 2016 將於 5 月 13 日在中央研究院人文社會科學館舉行，議程將涵蓋開放政府、開放資料及運用新科技公民運動等內容，預計參與人數約 750 人。

Who Spoke at g0v Summit 2014

[6 avatars]

Returning for a second year after the 2014 summit, the conference will focus on impact of ... interdisciplinary .... 

g0v is a civic tech community established in late 2012 with deep open-source roots. With more than 1,000 contributors and 30+ hackathons, it is recognized as one of the largest group in the global civic tech community.  The results are various civic-made tools for better information disclosure, citizen engagement, and online democracy.  Since the 2014 Summit, we’ve seen increasing collaboration between the civic tech communities in Taiwan, government agencies, media, and NGOs. People from all kinds of organizations have brought valuable data and thoughts into these collaborations. In turn, they often return to their organizations inspired by the open culture.

Here are some highlights of ...:

  • During the dust explosion accident, the civic tech community mobilized and work with the official emergency response team to provide timely information, and started digital disaster response drills.
  • The NGO alliance for constitution reform proposed several projects in the hackathon for people to better understand the issue.
  • Minister Tsai, who spoke at the last summit, proposed a consultation platform focused on digital regulations in a hackathon which turned into the vtaiwan platform [link to story]. A framework for evaluating online democracy tool called "blulu" is also emerging.
  • Taiwan topped the OKFN Open Data Index in 2015.  Besides vibrant community pushing for more data to be released, one of the main reasons is the open-definition compliant Open Government Data License enacted, which was drafted in the community.
