TEDxTaipei 2013 Intro

Kirby Wu - One of g0v.tw’s founding members.  A software engineer and web developer who loves hiking and photography.

In 0ct., 2012, the 45-second goverment-sponsored "Ecnomocis Boosting" advertisement for economic policies was aired. While it costs 4.5 million NT dollars, it conveyed an obfuscating message that it’s not citizens’ business to understand what the government is doing.  The first g0v hackathon was borne out of frustration on 2012.12.1 and attracted more people than expected.  A booming community was ignited

g0v.tw devotes both in promoting open data and developing software platforms for civic participation in public affair. By replacing the "o" in gov with "0", g0v represents the digital generation’s reassessment about the role of government.  Based on open-source software collaboration models, g0v involves in freedom of speech and information by providing citizens information services which are easy to use.

Information transparency helps citizens better understand how government works and public issues, so they can make informed judgement not just based on mass media, participate in governance, and ultimately, build a more democratic society.
